Who We Are

Visit GA is a not-for-profit organization that showcases and displays different sites to see and activities to do throughout Georgia. Georgia is the ideal getaway for you and your family with many things to do, from a picnic in Piedmont Park to camping in the Blue Ridge Mountains! The possibilities are endless here in the beautiful Peach State and that's why we're here to help!
